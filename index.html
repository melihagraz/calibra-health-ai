Calibretion examples

Method: split / inductive conformal prediction. A logistic-regression model is fit on a training split; a disjoint calibration split provides nonconformity scores (Vovk p-values for the class set; normalized residuals for the risk interval), giving distribution-free, finite-sample coverage close to the 90% target. This exact model runs client-side in this page.
